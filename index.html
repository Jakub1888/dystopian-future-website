Dystopian Fiction

Dystopian Fiction portrays a disturbing vision of a society or community in the near future. Main themes are governmental opression, control of population through technology and loss of individualism.

This form of literary fiction was heavily popularized in the 20th century as a subgenre of Science and Speculative Fiction and eventually found its way to the other forms of media in the Popular Culture of today.
